About Us

The Islamic Center Of Round Rock (ICRR) was founded in 2002 and is a 501 (c) non-profit organization located in Round Rock, Texas.

ICRR provides prayer services for the 5 daily prayers and the Friday prayer. ICRR has special programs for Ramadan including Iftar, Daily Taraweeh and arrangements for Aitikaf. ICRR also holds various halaqas and programs for the community (please refer to programs page for details). Click here for ICRR bylaws.
